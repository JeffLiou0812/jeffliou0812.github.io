Services

I take on the following types of projects as an independent professional. All engagements are project-based or hourly; scope and fees are confirmed in writing after an initial conversation.

1. Cross-Border Tax Consulting

International tax issues for multinational groups and investment structures: OECD Pillar Two / GloBE impact assessments, holding structure reviews, and tax analysis of dividend and cash repatriation routes.

Best fit: mid-size group finance teams, family offices, and project collaboration with accounting and law firms.

2. Corporate Training and Speaking

Training and talks on international tax topics: Pillar Two from fundamentals to implementation, cross-border tax for US stock investors, and how to read the income tax footnote in financial statements. Content tailored to the audience, delivered in English or Chinese.

Formats: half-day or full-day workshops, online courses, conference talks.

3. AI Research Automation Advisory

Helping individuals and teams build AI-assisted research workflows: Python data pipelines (SEC EDGAR, market data APIs), AI document deep-reading process design, and multi-source verification mechanisms against hallucination. Automate the repetitive work; keep human effort on judgment.

Deliverables: workflow design + scripts + documentation + handover training.

4. Content Collaboration and Commissioned Writing

Writing at the intersection of tax, US stocks, and AI automation; podcast and video guest appearances; co-produced content. English and Chinese both available.

Media, finance platforms, and education institutions welcome.

Scope boundaries: I do not provide buy/sell recommendations on individual securities, discretionary account management, or licensed investment advisory services. Tax consulting covers general structural and regulatory analysis; formal tax opinions and filing attestations belong with an accounting firm. Case-specific legal questions will be referred to qualified counsel.
